Find answers to common questions about our services

Ricas IT Services offers two main service categories: ICT Education and Software Development. Our ICT Education services include courses for kids, teens, and adults in areas such as basic computer skills, programming, web design, and more. Our Software Development services include custom web applications, mobile apps, school management systems, e-commerce solutions, and other tailored software solutions for businesses.

To enroll in an ICT education course, you can contact us through this contact form, email, or phone. Let us know which course you're interested in, and we'll provide you with information about upcoming sessions, pricing, and the enrollment process. We offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate different needs.

Our software development process typically includes the following steps: 1) Initial consultation and requirements gathering, 2) Planning and design, 3) Development, 4) Testing and quality assurance, 5) Deployment, and 6) Ongoing support and maintenance. We work closely with clients throughout the process to ensure the final product meets their specific needs and expectations.

The cost of our services varies depending on the specific requirements of each project or course. For ICT education, we offer different pricing tiers based on the course content, duration, and format. For software development, we provide custom quotes based on the scope, complexity, and timeline of the project. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.
